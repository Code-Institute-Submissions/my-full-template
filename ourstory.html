Our Story

It all started when a coffee enthusiast travelled around the world in search of the planets most respected coffee flavours. It was a journey into the uncharted and unknown world of exotic blends. On return to Ireland our coffee traveller wanted to share what he had discovered; to bring to limerick the tastes he had unearthed - the secrets of high High Grade Speciality Coffee.

He started with a modest coffee machine serving up delectable brews to the ever-growing coffee-curious public in a small market stand on Saturday mornings; from these humble beginnings, he expanded and bought a Giesen 6kg Small Batch Coffee Roaster. This allowed him to roast self-sourced Green Coffee from around the globe locally in Limerick.

Today Yellow Door Coffee source from only accredited high-grade speciality coffee from farms & brokers from suppliers in Tanzania and Brazil and everywhere in between where great coffee beans are grown. We do our best to offer quality coffee and interesting wines and deliver it with consistency and care to our customers. All the products we provide are all based on a simple ethos of quality, taste and environmental sustainability. We hope to build a strong coffee community, delivering excellence and laying the foundation for a sustainable planet.
