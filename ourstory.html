Our Story

It all started when a coffee enthusiast was travelling around the world...there was so many flavors and blends to experience that were unknown. On return to Ireland with an even stronger love of Coffee, we wanted Limerick to experience the different tastes of High Grade Speciality Coffee. We Started with a humble coffee machine serving up delectable brews to the ever growing coffee curious public in a small market stand on Saturday mornings., with this support we went on to expand our operation with a Giesen 6kg Small Batch Coffee Roaster ableing us to roast Self sourced Green Coffee from around the globe under the same roof.From Tanzania to Brazil we Source from only accredited High Grade Speciality Coffee Farms & Brokers.We do our utmost to offer quality coffee, interesting wines and deliver it as consistently and deliciously as possible to our customers. All of the products we offer are all based on simple ethos of quality, tastiness and environmental sustainability.We hope to build a strong community who will settle for no less than excellence and be kind to our planet in the process.
